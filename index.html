The Incident

On July 10, 2024, we visited the Japali Temple in Tirumala, Tirupati. Unfortunately, a terrible accident occurred where a large trunk fell on Uma Rani, causing severe injuries. She was immediately admitted to Sri Venkateshwara Institute of Medical Science (SVIMS), thanks to the quick actions of the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanam’s board (TTD). TTD assisted us with immediate medical assistance and have been incredibly supportive, ensuring she receives a critical spinal cord surgery that helped her to come out of danger zone for free of cost. However , due to the multiple injuries in the spinal cord, she is now paralyzed and there are chances of recovery if we take the right care post the surgery

Our Situation

We are now facing the enormous challenge of her post-surgery recovery and the associated medical expenses for her recovery back to normal.
